Synopsis

Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith revolves around the culmination of the Clone Wars and the fall of the Jedi Order. Chancellor Palpatine, secretly the Sith Lord Darth Sidious, manipulates events to consolidate his power over the Republic. Anakin Skywalker, a promising Jedi Knight, struggles with his allegiance to the Jedi Order and his fear of losing his loved ones. Seduced by promises of power and the ability to save his wife Padmé Amidala from death.

Chancellor Palpatine's grip on power tightens, while the Jedi Order faces challenges from within and without. Anakin Skywalker, a promising Jedi Knight, grapples with his emotions and loyalty as he confronts a series of profound challenges.
